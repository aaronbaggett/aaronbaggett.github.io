About

This site has been a long-time-coming. I've dabbled in Wordpress a bit before and could never seem to find an effecient theme or web host. During my Ph.D. program at Baylor Univeristy I began experimenting with GitHub. I learned about GitHub Pages and quickly realized the potential for publishing flexible, creative, and user-designed content.

I really like the content-focused delivery of Jekyll. I wanted the ability to write posts and author content in Markdown, push it all to GitHub, while avoiding so-called blogging software.

I came across Jonathan McGlone's site after browsing through a few examples powered by Jekyll. I really liked the minimal style and format. So much so that I decided to fork his repo and customize it to suit my needs. Thanks, Jonathan!
